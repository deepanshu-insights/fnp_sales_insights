# fnp_sales_insights

* Worked with a multi-table dataset covering customers, orders, and products to investigate sales trends, buying behavior, and product performance across festive occasions like Diwali, Holi, and Valentine's Day.
* Computed key business metrics including total revenue, average delivery time, customer spending average, and occasion-wise revenue to support strategic decision-making.
* Ranked the top 10 revenue-generating products and top 10 cities by order volume, while breaking down category-level performance and time-of-day revenue patterns (morning, afternoon, evening).
* Designed an interactive Excel dashboard with pivot tables and charts to visualize monthly revenue trends for 2023 and map product popularity across different gifting occasions.
